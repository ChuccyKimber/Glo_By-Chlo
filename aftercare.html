Sacramento raised, certified in tooth gem application, and fully convinced that a smile is the best place to keep your jewelry.

The vibe is 2003 sleepover, the standards are clinical. You'll leave with a sparkle you can't stop checking in your phone camera and an aftercare card so it stays that way.
