Real questions

Quick answers

Do tooth gems damage your teeth?
No. Gems are bonded to the surface of the enamel with the same class of dental composite used for orthodontic brackets — no drilling, no holes. Professionally removed, they leave nothing behind.
How long do they last?
Typically 3–12 months depending on placement, habits, and aftercare. Anything that pops off within the first 30 days gets replaced free.
Does it hurt?
Not even a little. There's no drilling and no needles — most clients say the hardest part is holding still while smiling.
